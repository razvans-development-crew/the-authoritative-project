the authoritative project (aka. 112x4 v3)
=========================================>>

a high-security, performance-oriented global ban manager and
remote administration toolkit for script builders written in
pure typescript and luau, supercharged by bun and powered by 
elysia and discord.js

-> for appeals, go to https://112x4.scriptlang.com/appeals
-> the privacy policy is at https://112x4.scriptlang.com/privacy
-> terms and conditions are at https://112x4.scriptlang.com/terms
